About Chain Collective

Chain Collective is dedicated to bridging the gender gap in Web3 by providing a robust ecosystem for African women. We believe in the transformative power of decentralized technologies and are committed to ensuring women across the continent are at the forefront of this revolution.

Our Mission

To empower African women with the knowledge, skills, and network to thrive and lead in the Web3 space, building upon the global movement for **financial inclusion and gender equity** championed by communities like Women in DeFi.

Our Vision

To be the leading platform for African women contributing to and shaping the decentralized future.

Inspired by a Movement: Our Roots in Women in DeFi

Chain Collective was born from the powerful vision and foundational work laid by Women in DeFi. We are deeply inspired by their unwavering commitment to financial literacy, empowerment, and closing the gender gap in the decentralized finance space.
